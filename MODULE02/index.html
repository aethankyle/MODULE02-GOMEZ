Yeng Guiao thanks ROS old-timers help in adjusting to system

Coach Yeng Guiao is happy that three of Rain Or Shine's players from its last championship team in 2016 have remained with the team until now, giving him needed help in adopting his system as he makes his way back to the squad. "Malaking bagay na nakatulong yung presence ni (It's a huge thing to have the presence of) Beau [Belga], Gabe [Norwood], and Jewel [Ponferada] because they've been with me in 2016," Guiao said on CNN Philippines Sports Desk program on Tuesday while he was conducting his first practice back as head coach.
